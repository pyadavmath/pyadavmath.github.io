About Me

I am a mathematics researcher and educator with a Master's degree in Mathematics. My primary research focus lies in Partial Differential Equations (PDEs), specifically nonlinear equations, Lie symmetry analysis, and numerical schemes for differential equations. Additionally, I am passionate about mathematics education, specializing in tutoring Grade 11/12, IB Mathematics, and Digital SAT curricula.
