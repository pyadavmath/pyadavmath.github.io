Summary & Overview

I am a PhD Research Scholar under the supervision of Dr. Sudarshan Santra in Department of Mathematics at BITS Pilani. My current research focuses on developing numerical solutions for fractional partial differential equations (PDEs) and integro-fractional differential equations.


I hold a strong foundational background in Lie symmetry analysis, Lie groups, and manifolds applied to nonlinear PDEs. I am passionate about bridging theoretical mathematics with practical, computational solutions, leveraging tools such as MATLAB, Mathematica, Maple, and Python to model and visualize complex systems.
