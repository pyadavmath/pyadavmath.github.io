About Me

I am a PhD Research Scholar under the supervison of Dr. Sudarshan Santra in Department of Mathematics at BITS Pilani. My current research focuses on developing numerical solutions for fractional PDEs. I hold a strong foundational background in Lie symmetry analysis, Lie groups, and manifolds, particularly applied to nonlinear PDEs. I am passionate about bridging theoretical mathematics with practical, computational solutions, leveraging tools such as MATLAB, Mathematica, Maple, and Python to model and visualize complex systems.
